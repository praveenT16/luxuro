Our Philosophy

Built Upon Wood, Clay, & Devotion.

At Luxora, we believe furniture shouldn't just fill space; it should sculpt it. Every curve, peg, and joint is calculated to deliver timeless poise to modern environments.

"Simplicity is not the lack of clutter, but the presence of clarity."

Our wood is sourced from FSC-certified Scandinavian forests. Handcrafted in our studio, each object requires weeks of attention by master carpenters trained in traditional Japanese joinery techniques.

The Luxora Standard

Committed to Excellence

Sustainable Forestry

We use only FSC-certified oak and walnut wood, harvested from forests where growth exceeds timber extraction rates.

Lifetime Warranty

We stand behind our woodworking. Each piece features a lifetime structural warranty covering all timber joinery joints.

Handcrafted Processes

No industrial automated assembly lines. Every chair is individual, built from start to end by a single master joiner.

Worldwide Delivery

We deliver to over 35 countries with bespoke shockproof packaging, and optional white-glove installation services.

How We Work

The Journey of Creation

01 / consultation

Personal Aesthetics Study

We align on spatial floor plans, lighting patterns, custom dimensions, and wood preferences to craft a personalized digital mood board.

02 / design

Scale Visualization

Our architecture team generates precise detailed drafts and material renders, ensuring every custom joint integrates into your floor layout.

03 / crafting

Millimeter Precision Crafting

Using centuries-old mortise-and-tenon joints, a dedicated master craftsman shapes FSC-certified lumber, finishing with organic organic oils.

04 / delivery

White Glove Setup

Our logistical team delivers and mounts your items inside shockproof wooden packaging crates, including on-site leveling checkup.
